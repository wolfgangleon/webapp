CrossRoads is a web application that shows information about thousands of outdoor recreation points of interest including trails, campgrounds, and ski resorts. Find photos, location, and descriptions of every place you looked for.

All of our trails data comes from www.trailAPI.com Copyright 2012 TrailAPI.

If you're interested in adding data to their data base go to Blue Spruce Ventures and follow their directions.
